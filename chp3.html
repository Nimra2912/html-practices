scrambled eggs

eggs are of my favourite foods. here is a recipe for deliciously riched scrambled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

method

  1. melt butter in a fry pan over a medium heat
  2. gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add egg and cream
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
